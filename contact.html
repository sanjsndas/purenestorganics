Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out our contact form, calling our office, or visiting our showroom. Our team will work with you to find a convenient time for your consultation. Consultations typically last 60-90 minutes and include a detailed assessment of your space and gardening needs. We recommend preparing any questions you have in advance to make the most of your consultation time.

2. What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the New York metropolitan area, including all five boroughs of New York City, Long Island, Westchester County, and parts of New Jersey and Connecticut. For locations outside our standard service area, additional travel fees may apply. Please contact us to discuss your specific location and we'll let you know if we can accommodate your needs.

3. How soon can you start my project?

Project start times vary depending on our current schedule and the complexity of your project. During peak seasons (spring and early summer), we recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance. For smaller projects or maintenance services, we may be able to accommodate you within 1-2 weeks. Contact us for current availability and we'll provide an estimated timeline for your specific project.

4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), checks, and bank transfers. For larger projects, we may require a deposit to secure your booking, with the balance due upon completion of services. We also offer financing options through third-party providers for qualifying customers. All payment details will be clearly outlined in your service agreement.

5. Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we offer emergency services for urgent plant health issues or irrigation system failures. Our emergency service line is available 24/7 at (123) 456-7891. Emergency service calls are subject to higher rates and availability. For non-emergency issues, we recommend scheduling during regular business hours to avoid additional fees.

6. Can I get a quote online?

We provide preliminary estimates based on the information you provide through our contact form or during a phone consultation. However, for accurate pricing, we typically need to assess your space in person. Our quotes are detailed and include all anticipated costs with no hidden fees. We're happy to discuss budget considerations and work with you to develop a plan that meets your needs and financial parameters.

7. What's your cancellation policy?

We require at least 48 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling of appointments. Cancellations made with less than 48 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the scheduled service cost. No-shows will be charged the full service fee. We understand emergencies happen and will consider exceptions on a case-by-case basis with proper documentation.

8. Do you provide maintenance services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance services for potted gardens, including watering, pruning, fertilizing, pest management, and seasonal plant rotation. Our maintenance services use only organic products and sustainable practices to keep your plants healthy and thriving.

10. What types of plants do you recommend for indoor gardens?

For indoor gardens, we recommend low-maintenance plants that thrive in various lighting conditions. Some popular choices include pothos, snake plants, peace lilies, and ZZ plants. These plants are not only beautiful but also help purify the air. We can help you select the best plants based on your space, light availability, and personal preferences during your consultation.
